\title{Re-analysing the data from Moffatt et al.~(2020): A textbook illustration of the fallacy of the null hypothesis}

\date{}

\abstract{
Moffatt et al.~(2020) reported the results of an experiment (N = 26 in the final sample) comparing the facial (surface) electromyographic correlates of induced rumination and distraction, following a stressful event. Based on the observation\ldots{} We show ou this conclusion seems at best hasty\ldots{} Indeed, concluding on the null, relation between self-reports and physiological measures\ldots{} Although the question Moffatt et al.~(2020) pursued if of interest, and mehods\ldots{} it is unclear whether this contribution really adds to the current knowledge\ldots{} Complete source code, reproducible analyses, and figures are available at \url{https://github.com/lnalborczyk/inner_experience_EMG}.
}
